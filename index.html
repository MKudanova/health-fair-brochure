About the Fair

Everyone deserves a fair chance at good health. Our Community Health & Wellness Fair brings trusted providers and resources directly to the neighborhood — no insurance required. Come for free screenings and vaccines, stay for cooking demos, fitness tips, and friendly conversations with local professionals.

Why attend?

  • Catch health issues early with quick, free checks.
  • Get vaccines and learn practical steps for everyday wellness.
  • Connect with insurance assistance and community programs.

What’s Happening

Free Screenings Blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol, BMI
Dental & Vision Quick assessments and referrals
Vaccinations Flu & COVID-19 (while supplies last)
Nutrition & Fitness Cooking demos, label-reading, mini-workouts
Healthy Tastings Try easy, budget-friendly recipes
Resource Booths Insurance, Medicaid/Medicare, housing & more

Get Involved
